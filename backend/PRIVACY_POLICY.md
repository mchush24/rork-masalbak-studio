# Privacy Policy for Zuna

**Last Updated:** November 24, 2025
**Effective Date:** November 24, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Zuna ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform (collectively, the "Service").

Zuna is an AI-powered child drawing analysis and psychological insights platform designed for parents and guardians. We are committed to protecting the privacy of both parents and the children whose information is shared with us.

**IMPORTANT NOTICE:** Zuna is designed for use by adults (parents and guardians) only. Users must be 18 years or older to create an account. While our Service involves analyzing children's drawings, we collect this information through parents/guardians, not directly from children.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 6. Children's Privacy

### 6.1 COPPA Compliance (United States)

Zuna is NOT directed to children under 13. Our Service is designed for use by adults (parents and guardians) only. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

**How We Protect Children's Data:**
- Only parents/guardians (18+) can create accounts
- We collect children's information (age, drawings) only through parents
- Parents maintain full control over their child's information
- Parents can delete their child's data at any time

If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@zuna.app.

### 6.2 GDPR-K Compliance (European Union)

For EU users, we implement enhanced protections for children's data:

**Parental Responsibility:**
- Account holders must be 18 or older
- Parents/guardians act as data controllers for their children's information
- Verifiable parental consent is obtained through account creation (18+ requirement)

**Data Minimization:**
- We collect only the minimum data necessary (child's age and drawings)
- No direct identifiers of the child (no name, photo, or personal details required)
- Analysis is pseudonymized and associated with the parent's account

**Age-Appropriate Processing:**
- Analysis and content are tailored to the child's developmental stage (ages 1-18)
- Clear, age-appropriate explanations for parents about how data is used

### 6.3 KVKK Compliance (Turkey)

As a Turkish-based service, we comply with KVKK (Law No. 6698):

- **Explicit Consent:** Parents provide explicit consent when creating accounts and uploading drawings
- **Information Obligation:** This Privacy Policy serves as our KVKK-required disclosure
- **Data Security:** Technical and administrative measures protect children's data
- **Parent Rights:** Parents can exercise all data subject rights on behalf of their children

### 6.4 Important Clarifications

**Not a Diagnostic Tool:**
Our Service provides general psychological insights for educational and developmental purposes only. It is NOT:
- A substitute for professional psychological evaluation
- Medical, therapeutic, or diagnostic advice
- Intended to diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition

**Parental Responsibility:**
Parents and guardians are solely responsible for:
- Deciding what drawings to upload
- Interpreting and acting on AI-generated insights
- Seeking professional help if concerned about their child's development
- Supervising their child's interaction with generated content (stories)

## 7. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

### 7.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption:** Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for admin access
- **Secure APIs:** Authentication-protected tRPC endpoints
- **Environment Variables:** API keys and credentials stored securely, never in code

### 7.2 Administrative Safeguards
- Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis
- Regular security training for team members
- Incident response procedures
- Regular security audits and updates

### 7.3 Data Storage
- **Database:** Supabase PostgreSQL with row-level security
- **Images:** Supabase Storage with access controls
- **Backups:** Automated, encrypted backups
- **Logging:** Secure audit logs for monitoring

### 7.4 Limitations
No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Data Retention

### 8.1 Retention Periods

**Account Data:**
- Retained while your account is active
- Deleted within 90 days of account deletion request

**Drawing Images:**
- Retained while your account is active
- You can delete individual drawings at any time
- All drawings deleted within 30 days of account deletion

**Analysis Results:**
- Retained for development tracking purposes while account is active
- Deleted within 90 days of account deletion

**Generated Stories:**
- Retained for 90 days after generation (unless account is deleted sooner)
- Can be deleted individually by users at any time

**Backup Data:**
- Backups retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted
- Deleted data removed from backups after 30-day backup cycle completes

### 8.2 Legal Retention

We may retain certain information longer if required by law or to resolve disputes, enforce agreements, or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention). Such retained data will be minimized and secured.

### 8.3 Anonymized Data

We may retain anonymized, aggregated data indefinitely for research and service improvement. This data cannot be used to identify individuals.

**Right to Restriction:**

**Right to Object:**
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests

**Right to Withdraw Consent:**
